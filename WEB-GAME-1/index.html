KIYO

In the distant future, Earth’s atmosphere is collapsing, and fragments of energy shards are falling from the sky.

You are KIYO, the last shard runner—an elite explorer tasked with collecting power shards to restore the planet’s energy core.

Dodge falling debris, collect energy shards, and level up your abilities to reach the final level, where you’ll face the Shatterstorm—the last trial to save the world.
